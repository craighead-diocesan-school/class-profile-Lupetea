Nau Mai, Haere Mai Ki Flexigrid Highschool!

In Flexigrid Digital Technology Highschool we have an all girls classes for years 11-13 who aim to provide all students with an opportunity of a good learning and supportive experience. We are located in the South Island, Timaru on Flexigrid Avenue. We specialise in Digital Technology, as we push and motivate our students to accomplish and reach their dreams and aspirations going above and beyond. Whether it may be to become an Information Technology Architect, Software Developer, Digital marketing or Game Developer or whatever may interest them, we are all here for it in assisting you to achieve and reach your full purpose and potential.
